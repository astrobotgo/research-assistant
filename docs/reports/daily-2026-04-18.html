Dark Matter 6 papers

Recent submissions explore various theoretical frameworks for dark matter. One study investigates echoes of global cosmic strings and their detectability through cosmological probes like the CMB, suggesting a potential role for Nambu-Goldstone bosons as dark matter [4]. Another paper delves into inelastic self-interacting dark matter, showing how internal dynamics can suppress small-scale structure formation and produce observably different power spectra [5]. Additionally, primordial black holes are explored as macroscopic dark matter candidates via higher-dimensional models that allow for massive growth during early Universe epochs [6].
